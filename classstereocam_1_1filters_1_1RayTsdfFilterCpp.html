Detailed Description

Per-ray TSDF fusion on the organized cloud (organized-cloud stage).

A truncated running mean that collapses the TSDF to the surface along each ray. _D/_W are internal state — cleared via reset(). A point whose Z is within _trunc metres of the fused Z is averaged in (weight ramps to _max_weight); one outside restarts that ray; holes are filled from history. Validated in examples/python/ray_tsdf_validate.py.
