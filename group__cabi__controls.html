DEPZ_API depz_status depz_list_filter_controls ( const char *  filter_slug,
depz_control *  out_array,
int32_t  max_count,
int32_t *  out_count 
)

Enumerate the controls of a built-in filter into a caller-supplied array.

Writes min(found, max_count) entries; *out_count receives the total found (may exceed max_count — re-allocate and retry). Pass max_count=0 to just query the count. filter_slug ∈ {"speckle","zclip","edge","ray_tsdf","temporal_ema","temporal_kalman","bbox","stat_outlier"}.

Returns
DEPZ_OK on success; DEPZ_ERR_UNKNOWN_PARAM for an unknown slug.
