Detailed Description

IParameterStore view over a StereoCalibration.

Loads a calibration JSON and exposes its scalars and matrices — camera matrices, distortion coefficients, rotation/translation, image size, baseline, focal length and \(Q\) matrix — as keyed ParamValue entries for generic parameter-driven consumers.
