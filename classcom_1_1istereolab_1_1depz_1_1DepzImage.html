Detailed Description

An immutable, copied-out view of one frame stream.

The bytes are copied from the native buffer at read time, so a

DepzImage

stays valid after its DepzFrame is closed.

U8 streams (raw / rectified BGR images) expose bytes(); F32 streams (disparity / depth / point cloud) expose floats().
