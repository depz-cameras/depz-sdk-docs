Detailed Description

Pinhole stereo IDepthCalibration: rectify a raw pair onto the inference canvas.

Wraps StereoCalibration; prepare() builds the canvas-fit remap and returns the rescaled CanvasSetup scalars, then rectify() resamples each raw eye through the cached maps.
