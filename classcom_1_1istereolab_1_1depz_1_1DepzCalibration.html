Detailed Description

Stereo calibration loaded from JSON, with read-only identity scalars.

Standalone wrapper for inspection — DepzPipeline.Builder loads its own calibration internally, so a pipeline does not need one of these.

Undistort + stereo-rectify a native-resolution pair so that corresponding points share a scanline (epipolar lines become rows):

\[ \tilde{p} = K\, R\, K^{-1}\, \mathrm{undistort}(p) \]

with \(K\) the intrinsics and \(R\) the per-eye rectifying rotation. Call prepare first (a fresh calibration has no maps).

Parameters
left,rightRaw native-resolution eye buffers.
leftWidth,leftHeightSize of the left eye.
rightWidth,rightHeightSize of the right eye.
channelsChannel count (e.g. 3 for BGR).
Returns
The rectified pair (equal-size buffers + their width/height).
