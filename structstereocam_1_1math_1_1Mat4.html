Detailed Description

4×4 matrix of float in column-major (OpenGL) storage.

Supports affine and projective transforms of Vec3:

  • Matrix product \(C = A B\)
  • Point transform (homogeneous \(w = 1\), perspective divide)
  • Direction transform (homogeneous \(w = 0\))
  • Factory constructors for translation, scale, rotation, look-at and perspective projection.

Perspective projection matrix (right-handed, depth in \([-1, 1]\)).

\[ M = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{1}{a\,t} & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & \frac{1}{t} & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & -\frac{f+n}{f-n} & -\frac{2 f n}{f-n} \\ 0 & 0 & -1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}, \quad t = \tan\!\tfrac{\text{fovy}}{2} \]

Parameters

[in]nearNear clipping distance \(n\).
[in]farFar clipping distance \(f\).

◆ transformPoint()

Vec3 stereocam::math::Mat4::transformPoint ( const Vec3 &  v) const
inline

Transform a point (homogeneous \(w = 1\)), with perspective divide.

\[ \begin{bmatrix} x' \\ y' \\ z' \\ w' \end{bmatrix} = M \begin{bmatrix} v_x \\ v_y \\ v_z \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}, \qquad \mathbf{p}' = \frac{1}{w'}\,(x', y', z') \]

The divide is skipped when \(w' \in \{0, 1\}\).
