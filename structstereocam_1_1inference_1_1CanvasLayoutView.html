Detailed Description

Per-frame letter/pillar-box layout of the valid image inside the canvas.

pad_x/pad_y is the top-left of the valid (post-rectify) region inside the padded inference canvas; valid_w × valid_h is its size. Mirrors calibration::CanvasLayout, redefined here to keep this header free of calibration includes.
